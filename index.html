Department of Computer Engineering

The department of Computer Engineering at PCCoE is committed for value added quality technical education in Computer Engineering. The strong pillars of our department for UG and PG program are the State of the Art Infrastructure, Competent Faculty and hardworking students.

Program Educational Objectives

Graduates of the Programme will leverage Engineering fundamentals in their successful careers.Graduates of the Programme will exhibit innovation and technological competence in computing all through their careers.
