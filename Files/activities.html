NSS Coordinator

National Service Scheme comes under the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India and NSS cell, Higher and technical education, Govt. of Maharashtra. NSS was launched in Gandhiji's Centenary year, 1969-70. Understanding the need of social development of youth from technical field to serve the society in effective way, NSS was started in the academic year 2008-09 in Pimpri Chinchwad College of Enginering, Nigdi. The scheme aims to inculcate social welfare in students, and to provide better service to society through voluntary work. NSS volunteers work to ensure that everyone who is needy gets help to enhance their standard of living. While working for the society volunteers learn how to perform in a team effectively, how to various social activities and how to lead a good life despite a scarcity of resources.

Google’s Applied CS with Android

Applied CS with Android is a course designed by Google to strengthen the core CS concepts of college students. It is held across multiple colleges and universities across the globe. The Applied CS workshop was initiated in PCCOE on 8th September, 2017. There will be a total of 7-8 sessions where the participants will be building Android apps using various data structures ranging from HashMaps to Graphs and AVL trees. Since Google recommends that the workshop have a small number of highly dedicated participants only, the students were selected across all divisions on recommendation of the respective class teachers.

Workshop on Microsoft Azure

This is the first ever workshop which was conducted by the MSP community of PCCOE. This workshop started with spreading awareness about MSP program, Microsoft Virtual Academy (MVA) and Imagine Cup and then continued with an introduction to Microsoft Azure. In this workshop, students were made aware of various useful Microsoft Education resources and were made ready to jump-start with Azure.
Contents covered were:

  • Introduction to Microsoft Student Partner program.
  • Awareness of free learning resources provided by Microsoft.
  • Awareness on Microsoft Imagine Cup.
  • An introduction to Microsoft Azure.
