Professional Chapters

ACM

Set up in 2012, the PCCOE ACM Student Chapter is one of the best Student Chapter in Pune. Year by year, the Chapter strives to increase the number of events, and the participation of students. With the guidance of the faculty sponsors, and the enthusiastic volunteer and participating students, the Chapter which is now in its eighth year is improving rapidly on all counts. With an aim to be the best Student Chapter in the country, we are rapidly taking long strides to meet the goal.

ACM-W

This chapter has been established with an aim to support and motivate the women in computing, and thereby bring about gender equality in this field. Although most of the events are organized for women, but this chapter also arranges events which help in the overall development of all the students. Besides that, ACM-W PCCOE encourages women to participate in the ACM-W Regional Celebrations, Grace Hopper Celebrations and all such similar events which are targeted for girls in the field of computing.
